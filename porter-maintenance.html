Porter, Site-Inspection & General Maintenance

Keep common areas clean and presentable, get consistent site documentation, safety issue reporting, and standardized HOA violation reporting as well as general maintenance services and project management and coordination.

Our Summit Site Shield program pairs dependable porter services with structured walkthroughs, photo documentation, ticketing, and manager-ready reporting. When appropriate, we can also handle general maintenance and project management and coordination.

Porter & Common-Area Presentation

A clean, orderly property reduces complaints, improves owner confidence, and helps your management team stay proactive. Our porter scope is focused on common-area presentation and light, non-hazardous cleaning.

  • Trash patrol and litter pickup (including documentation of illegal dumping when observed)
  • Sweep/blow breezeways, sidewalks, and entries
  • Cobweb removal in accessible areas
  • Spot clean visible, non-hazardous spills/stains
  • Amenity/common-area wipe downs as directed by management
  • Many other tasks inquire for full listing
  • See-it / report-it photo documentation of observed issues (with location notes)

Ticketing Workflow & Status Tracking

Work requests and observations can be converted into structured tickets so nothing falls through the cracks. Tickets are tracked with clear notes, photos, and outcomes.

  1. Request intake (email/text/portal) and ticket number assignment
  2. Scheduling window confirmation and site access coordination
  3. On-site documentation (photos + notes) and completion details
  4. Disposition: Completed / Deferred / Refer-out
  5. Closeout posted within 24 hours after completion (or after client-confirmed vendor completion)

Summit Site Shield Walkthroughs & Documentation

Beyond basic presentation, Site Shield adds a repeatable walkthrough and reporting program that creates a time-stamped photo log and an issue register your team can act on.

  • Relationship-appropriate walkthrough cadence (tailored by site size, hot spots, and seasonality)
  • Issue register with unique IDs and status tracking (Open / In Progress / Closed)
  • Lane routing recommendations: (A) porter correction, (B) incident ticket candidate, (C) refer-out
  • Close-out proof (photos and notes) when items are resolved
  • Optional weekly snapshots and board-ready monthly packets

General Maintenance & Landscape Maintenance

When a need arises, when identified through Site Shield walkthroughs or during porter work, or on a continuing basis, we can handle it as a discrete, pre-approved service ticket.

Common Maintenance Examples (inquire for full listing)

  • Minor door/hardware adjustments (tighten hinges/handles, replace doorstops on non-fire-rated doors)
  • Replace/repair window screens (re-screen, spline, minor frame tweaks)
  • Install or replace non-electrical signage/placards (management-approved locations)
  • Common-area touch-ups (small patch + touch-up paint; cosmetic caulk where appropriate)
  • Manual weeding / bed cleanup for presentation (general landscaping maintenance)
  • Surface-safe gum removal, graffiti wipe-down with approved non-hazardous cleaners

Major Project Coordination / Accountability

  • Site- Access and point of contact coordination
  • Documentation and Reporting, updates and progress tracking
  • Materials & Equipment organization and staging
  • Construction Site Clean-Up, pre, ongoing, and post
  • Project Management, coordination, quotes, walkthroughs
  • Verified Completion, including photos and notes

Who This Is Built For

  • HOAs that need reliable common-area presentation plus consistent documentation
  • Property managers overseeing multifamily communities with recurring hot spots
  • Commercial sites that benefit from scheduled walkthroughs and issue routing

How We Structure Service

  • Retainer for porter + Site Shield walkthroughs + Site-Inspection and reporting (cadence by site)
  • Tickets for approved items (porter corrections, documentation-only, general maintenance)
  • Refer-out coordination available (access scheduling + photo documentation) — client contracts directly

Deliverables You Can Expect

  • Time-stamped photo log grouped by category/location
  • Issue register with unique IDs, status, and lane routing
  • Close-out photos and notes
  • Optional weekly snapshot and board-ready monthly packet

Professional Boundaries You Can Rely On

  • We provide observational documentation and administrative routing — not legal advice or code enforcement
  • We do not bid as a contractor and do not perform licensed-trade or permit-required work
  • We do not inflate or adjust contracts without approved work-orders and tickets
  • We maintain professional boundaries, attire, and communication at all times
