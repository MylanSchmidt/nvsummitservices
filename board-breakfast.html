Fiduciary Duties of HOA Board Members

Matt Grode

HOA General Counsel | Board Governance & Legal Education

In his many years of practice, Mr. Grode has represented hundreds of community associations relative to a broad array of legal issues including, but not limited to, interpretation and enforcement of governing documents, drafting of governing documents, contract negotiations and disputes, and construction defect litigation. His practice includes the representation of commercial and residential owners, homeowners associations, and builders with regard to construction defect litigation. Mr. Grode frequently represents buyers, sellers, and brokers in residential real estate transactions and litigation. Additionally, Mr. Grode provides advice and counsel to numerous business entities with regard to their transactional and litigation matters.

Mediation- Following extensive training, Mr. Grode has served as the mediator in countless disputes. He also assisted in the creation of the Greater Los Angeles CAI Chapter mediation program.

MW

Insurance 101 for HOAs

Matt Watty

Farmers Insurance | HOA Insurance & Risk Management

Matthew Watty is a seasoned insurance professional with over 10 years of experience as an agent with Farmers Insurance and other carriers, specializing in community associations. A proud native of Nevada, Matthew combines his deep roots in the community with a comprehensive understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities within the region. Matthew has cultivated extensive expertise in the homeowner’s association industry, offering insurance solutions to meet the specific needs of communities. His knowledge of HOA policies, risk management, and compliance requirements positions him as a trusted advisor and educator in the field.

Matthew’s passion for educating others drives his desire to share his expertise with industry professionals through engaging and informative classes. His goal is to empower community managers with the knowledge and tools they need to navigate the complexities of insurance in Nevada, ensuring they can better serve their clients and communities.
